Properties of blood

• it is a heterogeneous mixture of solid cells and liquid plasma

• It has a specific gravity between 1.056 and 1.06

• It has a viscosity 3.5 to 5.5 times higher than water

• It accounts for 7-9% of the total body weight and has a volume of 5-6 liters

• It transports oxygen, carbon dioxide, nutrients, wastes, and immune cells
